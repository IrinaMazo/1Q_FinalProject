About?

This page aims to educate its readers/users on the pressing issues consumerism has in the environment. While consumerism may be ‘beneficial’ for the happiness of the consumers and the growth of the economy, people has made irresponsible purchases that now leads to the concerns and issues that will be tackled in this page.

What is consumerism?

Consumerism is the promotion and/or the encouragement of buying goods and services. It mainly functions under the interests or demands of the consumers and relies on trends.

Why is it important to note?
